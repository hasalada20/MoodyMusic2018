AAAA
The simplest style of playing. Play the whole chord all at once without any alteration.

ABAB
Switch between hitting just the root of the chord and the rest of it's notes back and forth.

ABBB
Hit the root of the chord once, then for the rest of it's play time, play all notes of the chord.

ABCD
Play the notes of each chord in an arpeggiated (one note at a time) fashion.
